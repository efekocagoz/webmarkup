Historical

This is perfect for those who love exploring the past, visiting ancient landmarks, and learning about the cultures that shaped the world. Destinations like Rome, Athens, or Egypt offer travelers a glimpse into history through iconic ruins and museums.

Adventurous

Adventurous is for thrill-seekers looking for adrenaline-pumping activities, like hiking in the Himalayas, scuba diving in the Great Barrier Reef, or zip-lining through rainforests. It's all about experiencing nature and challenges firsthand.

Relaxation

Relaxation focuses on unwinding and rejuvenating. Popular in tropical beach destinations or serene mountain resorts, these trips center around activities like spa treatments, sunbathing, and enjoying peaceful surroundings.
