Unique Border Quirk: At Baarle, a small town on the Belgium-Netherlands border, the boundary zigzags through houses, businesses, and even some dining tables, where people can be in two countries at once.

Longest Place Name: Ever been to Taumatawhakatangihangakoauauotamateaturipukakapikimaungahoronukupokaiwhenuakitanatahu in New Zealand? This tongue-twister is the longest place name in the world!

Passport Power: The Japanese passport is the most powerful in the world, allowing visa-free or visa-on-arrival access to 193 countries.

Sandboarding in the Desert: Forget snowboarding—did you know you can go sandboarding down the dunes in Peru’s Huacachina Desert or in the Sahara?
